## Decision

This is not a speed win. The quiet A/B is route-mismatched:

- control reached the opening scene and produced `82282490` body-time data;
- experiment stayed at loading and produced no `82282490` body-time data;
- neither run had searched fatal markers.

Keep `arm64_context_promotion_gpr_livein_r1` default-off. The audited
replacement-count success (`64/107`) is real, but it did not translate into a
usable quiet speed proof here.
