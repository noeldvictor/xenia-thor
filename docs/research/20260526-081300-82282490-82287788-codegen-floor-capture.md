## Decision

Do not patch generated behavior from this capture.

The capture supports keeping `arm64_offset_memory_address_fastpath` enabled in
future Blue Dragon speed captures because the prior same-APK A/B was route-clean
and locally positive. It does not open an immediate second addressing patch:
`82282490` is now dominated by a tiny scalar/context/CR block plus an already
closed VMX/stvewx/mul-add block, while `82287788` contributes little offset
traffic.

Do not rerun unchanged `82282490` / `82287788` HIR codegen-floor captures. The
next fastmem/addressing slice must be broader and prove a reusable rule, such
as a no-wrap / normal-memory eligibility audit for hot `LOAD_OFFSET` /
`STORE_OFFSET` forms across body-dominant route blocks. If that cannot produce
material, legal, route-wide candidates, close the current fastmem/addressing
lane and move to another structural A64 path.
