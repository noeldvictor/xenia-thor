## Decision

No generated-code behavior patch from this slice.

The previous `82287788` stack-slot `f[1]` carrier proved that the read-only
lane is dynamically real, but the quiet A/B did not prove speed. The new plan
says `f[1]` remains the only low-enough-risk forwarding candidate, but it
should move from a callee-local reload slot toward a function-pair/callee
variant design that can seed the value at entry while preserving required PPC
context visibility.

Do not shortcut `fpscr`, `r[3]`, or `lr` from this evidence. `fpscr` is the
largest static opportunity, but it is mutable and needs a real dirty-status
cache with exact writeback rules. `r[3]` is mutable state, and `lr` is
call-link state.

## Next Experiment

Design or audit a default-off `82282490 -> 82287788` function-pair/callee
variant before changing behavior:

- keep explicit kills/writebacks for unknown calls, indirect exits,
